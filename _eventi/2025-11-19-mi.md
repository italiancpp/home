### La sessione

**Managing complex development environments in Conan2**

When your software runs on a variety of different machines, architectures and products the task of managing and evolving SDKs, dependencies and development environments becomes a full time job. Let's discover how we can use Conan2 to help us during the whole development cycle (and not only as a dependency manager).
